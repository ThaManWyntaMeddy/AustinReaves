About Austin Reaves

Austin Reaves is an American professional basketball player who currently plays for the Los Angeles Lakers in the NBA. He played college basketball for the Wichita State Shockers and the Oklahoma Sooners.

Career Highlights

  • Joined the Los Angeles Lakers in 2021
  • Named to the All-Big 12 First Team in 2021
  • Averaged 18.3 points per game in his senior year at Oklahoma
